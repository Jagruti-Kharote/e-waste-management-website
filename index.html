What is E-Waste

E-Waste means discarded electrical or electronics devices.
These include old computers,mobile phones.
TVs,batteries,and cables. When not disposed of properly, they release toxic substances like mercury,lead,and cadmium that pollute our soil,air,and water.

Sources Of E-Waste


Homes- old phones, TVs, kitchen appliances.

Offices- Computers, printers, cables, and batteries.

Industries-Large machines and electronic tools.

Harmful Effects Of E-Waste

Causes environmental pollution(air, water, and soil contamination).
Leads to serious health problems like lung and skin diseases.
Wildlife and plants get affected due to chemical waste.

Need For Action

We must take immediate action to reduce e-waste proper recycling and safe disposal protect our environment and future generations Everyone can help by following the 3Rs- Reduce, Reuse, and Recycle.
